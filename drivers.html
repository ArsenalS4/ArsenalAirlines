MISSION BRIEFING

Ground vehicle operators provide tactical mobility, logistics, and fire-support transport across contested environments. Drivers must prioritize convoy discipline, threat awareness, vehicle survivability (armor and spacing), and rapid casualty extraction.

Maintain radio discipline, follow commander waypoints, and execute immediate evasive maneuvers on contact. Rules of engagement: keep vehicle moving when under fire unless ordered to dismount; protect dismount zones and prioritize passenger safety.
